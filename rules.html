What is Fifteen Puzzle?

  • A sliding puzzle that consists of a frame of numbered square tiles in random order with one tile missing.
  • The puzzle also exists in other sizes.
  • If the size is 3×3 tiles, the puzzle is called the 8-puzzle, if 4×4 tiles, the puzzle is called the 15-puzzle or 16-puzzle.

Objective

  • The objective of the game is to arrange the tiles in numerical order after it has shuffled.
  • Player can also arrange the tiles based on the background image chosen.
  • Player has to move a square repeatedly until it returns to its original position.

How to play the game on our website?

  • The game starts with an already arranged puzzle.
  • Player can choose a background from a collection of pictures to use.
  • Player can also choose a puzzle size other than 4x4.
  • Once the player is ready, he or she can click on the shuffle button to shuffle tiles randomly.
  • Once the shuffle button is hit, a timecounter starts running to keep track of the time the player takes to solve the puzzle.
  • There is another counter on the left side of the puzzle that counts the number of tile moves the player has made.
  • If the player wins, he/she will be notified and will have the option to start a new game from the navigation bar on the top of the page.
